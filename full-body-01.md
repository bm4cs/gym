# Full body 01

A remixed full body program at the start of 2020 (COVID).

## Monday

- back squats
- incline bench press
- seated row
- tricep pulldowns
- side raises

## Tuesday

- Pullups
- Deadlifts sumo
- Leg press
- Cable fly

## Wednesday

- Bench press
- Lat pull downs
- Lying leg curls
- Standing calf raises
- Abs and planks

## Thursday

- Overhead press
- Dumbbell bentover rows (lawn-mowers)
- Lunges

## Friday

- Deadlifts (heavy)
- Bench press
- Squats
